Facts About Me:

I am a passionate photographer with a love for capturing the beauty of nature. My work focuses on wildlife, birds, and the stunning landscapes that surround us.

Photography is not just a hobby for me; it's a way of life. I believe that every moment in nature tells a story, and I strive to capture those stories through my lens.

Most of my photos were taken in the Danube Delta, one of my favorite places to explore and observe birds in their natural habitat.

I currently shoot with a Canon 800D, which helps me capture the fine details and emotion in every scene. My goal is to inspire others to slow down, observe, and appreciate the small wonders that surround us every day.
